Overview

  • january 31, 2019
  • 3 comments
+

Set on Negril's popular Seven-Mile Beach, Grand Pineapple Beach Negril is the epitome of a laid-back, authentic Jamaican holiday. This intimate resort is set within lush, perfectly manicured gardens bursting with beautiful flowers and gently swaying palms. The Beachside Cottages are located a stone's throw from the beach, bar and restaurant while the Gardenside Manor is nestled in the gardens across the road. Close to Negril's entertainment zone where you can go exploring, but far enough so as to enjoy the quiet. The resort, with its authentic Caribbean architecture, caters to all tastes. Created to accommodate families, couples and singles, the resort offers a variety of activities as well; from kayaking and Sunfish sailing, to a beachfront open-air restaurant and evening entertainment three times a week — everything is included!

Rooms
+

65 rooms in a garden or beachside location on 2 floors. Gardenside Manor Rooms are located on the ground floor with air-conditioning, cable TV, WiFi access*, clock radio, safe, tea/coffee maker, iron and board and hairdryer. These rooms have 2 double beds, a communal patio and sleep maximum 3 adults or 2 adults and 1 child.

Fun and Games
+

Freshwater swimming pool located on the garden side of the resort. Whirlpool located on the beachside. Table tennis, beach volleyball, lawn chess and an assortment of board games. Kayaks, Sunfish sailing and windsurfing. Scuba-diving* and snorkelling*. Spa boutique with Caribbean-inspired treatments* on the garden side of the resort.
